Problem Statement

Given three sides of a triangle (i.e., x, y, and z), determine if the triangle can exist. If it can exist, determine the type of triangle.

Note: 

In order for a triangle to exist, the sum of the two shorter sides must be greater than than the long side.

An equilateral triangle is a triangle in which all three sides are equal.

A scalene triangle is a triangle that has three unequal sides.

An isosceles triangle is a triangle with (at least) two equal sides.

Sample Input 1

30
60
90

Sample Output 1

Does not exist

Sample Input 2

6
6
6

Sample Output 2

Equilateral triangle

Sample Input 3

9
9
6

Sample Output 3

Isosceles triangle

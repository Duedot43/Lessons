Your challenge is to create a simple 2-player rock paper scissors game:
- This is a 2 player game where each player chooses either Rock Paper or Scissors. The program will determine who the winner is based on what they picked
 
Your program should do the following:
 - Explain the game rules
- Ask for player names (and use those names later)
- Ask for the first player to make a choice while the second player looks away
- Ask the second player to make a choice while player one turns away
- Calculate who wins the game
- Respond with "(Player 1's name) picked ____", (Player 2's name) picked _____"
- Respond with who the winner is
 
 For this program - you will not be turning in the code, I would like you to take a video of you and someone else play your game and submit the video.  The video just needs to be of the screen and keyboard while you play.

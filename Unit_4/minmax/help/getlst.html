You can store parts of a list into a string by using list[number_you_want_to_get]
For example:
     #set our list      myList = ['spaces', 'in', 'HTML', 'are', 'hard']
     #with "spaces" being 0
#"in" being 1
#"HTML" being 2
#"are" being 3
#"hard" being 4
     #now store variable from "myList" into the variable word:
     word = myList[2]
     #now to print the variable word
     print(word)
     #you can also do
     print(myList[2])
     Output:
          HTML
